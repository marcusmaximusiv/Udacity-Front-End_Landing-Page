Red Bull Sports

Mike Swanson

The third generation skydiver made his name by taking gold multiple times in a discipline known in the early days as freeflying. Throughout his career he has won multiple gold medals, US national championship titles, and international championship titles

His drive, dedication and skill in skydiving has made him known as the undisputed king of the skies and has allowed him to explor other avenues of aerial sports

Jaylen Brown

Since being drafted in 2016 by the Boston Celtics the All-Star forward in the 2022 season has averaged 23 points per game and has had a field goal percentage of 47% and a 3 point percentage of 35% cementing why he is one of the leaders of the Boston Celtics.

Brown makes sure to reflect that leadership off the court by empowering kids, fighting racial injustice, and redefining what it means to be a star in the NBA today

Neymar

The Brazilian Legend started out at the Brazilian club Santos before departing for FC Barcelona where he teamed up with Football Greats Lionel Messi and Luis Suarez cementing his legacy as one of the best footballers of his generation. He now plays for Paris Saint German where he hopes to add more trophies, titles, and awards to his already illustrious career.

He is the host of Red Bull Neymar Jr's Five which is a fast, fun and thrilling competition where Neymar Jr seeks out the world's best street soccer stars.

Ninja

Ninja, also known as Tyler Blevins, is a champion "Fortnite" player. He also has more than 13 million followers on Instagram and Twitch and an additional 20 million subscribers on Youtube

The 27-year old Illinois native who dropped out of community college has learned to turn his extraordinary personality and considerable gaming talent into an unmatched, hugely profitable enterprise
